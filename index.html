ABOUT NIPPY

Nippy Print has established itself as a leader in providing total printing solutions for every business need. In the highly competitive environment of the 21st century. More often than not, it is the visual impact of your logo on your business stationery that makes the first and most lasting impression on your existing and prospective new clients.



From concept to completion, our focus is to deliver optimum impact and compelling design in the fastest possible turnaround time. Staffed by skilled print and design specialists we have the expertise to offer you a customised, cost-effective solution for your specific printing needs. We offer complete design, layout and typesetting for every project. From updating your current business forms to designing a promotional brochure, leaflet or mailshot from scratch, our business is enhancing the image of your business.

OUR SERVICES

PRE-RPINTING

  • Design and conceptualization of any item to be printed
  • Artwork and layout of any item to be printed
  • Typesetting
  • Page make-up
  • Scanning and CMYK reproduction
  • Computer to plate

FINISHING

  • Mechanised and hand folding
  • Mechanised and hand collating
  • Saddle stitching
  • Stapling
  • Perfect binding
  • Section sewing with covers drawn on
  • Case binding
  • Quarterbinding
  • Padding
  • Drilling or punching
  • Inserting
  • General hand assembly including gluing, making up of boxes and folders, stringing and eyeleting

LITHO PRINTING

  • Four and five colour printing utilising CMYK or an array of Pantone colours
  • Single and multi-colour printing utilizing CMYK or an array of Pantone colours
  • Duplicate or triplicate NCR books utilizing an in-line numbering and perforating process

DIGITAL PRINTING

  • Digital short-run printing
  • Silkscreen printing
  • Photo copies
  • Large format printing

POST PRESS FINISHING
